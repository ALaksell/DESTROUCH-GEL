Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Destrouch Gel

Is Destrouch Gel safe to use around children and pets?

+

While Destrouch Gel uses natural ingredients, we recommend keeping it out of reach of children and pets. Apply in areas that are not accessible to them, and always wash hands after handling the product.

How long does one tube of Destrouch Gel last?

+

One tube of Destrouch Gel contains enough product to treat an average-sized home (approximately 1,500 sq ft). The gel remains effective for up to 3 months after application.

How quickly will I see results?

+

Most customers report seeing a significant reduction in cockroach activity within 24-48 hours. Complete elimination typically occurs within 1-2 weeks, depending on the severity of the infestation.

Can I use Destrouch Gel in food preparation areas?

+

Yes, Destrouch Gel can be used in food preparation areas, but should be applied in cracks, crevices, and areas not directly in contact with food. Always ensure proper cleaning of surfaces before food preparation.
